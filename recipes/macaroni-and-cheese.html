Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Savory and creamy macaroni and cheese that's sure to satisfy the whole family! Prepare as a side dish or as quick lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 box (12 to 16 ounces) elbow macaroni
  • 1 box (8 ounces) cream cheese
  • 2 - 4 tablespoons butter
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put the cream cheese into a casserole dish so it softens while you boil the macaroni.
  2. Cook the elbow macaroni according to package directions. Rinse and drain well, then pour it into the casserole dish.
  3. Stir the cream cheese and macaroni together until well blended.
  4. Add butter, milk and shredded cheese. (Add these ingredients a little at a time so you can adjust the amounts to your preference.) Mix well.
  5. Top with grated Parmesan cheese and a little salt and pepper. Done!
